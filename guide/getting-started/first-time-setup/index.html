    First-Time Setup Guide¶

    Welcome to the realm! Before you can draw your blade, you need to manifest your character instance on the HUD. Follow these steps to configure your initial identity.

    Manifest Your Gear¶

    Before the Setup HUD can bind a character to you, wear the essentials:

    1. Feudalism 4 Players HUD — attached, not rezzed on the ground.
    2. A Feudalism pouch — extra storage and crafting aggregation (up to two on F4 personal-inventory HUDs).

    Some universes also expect a Meter, a drink skin or bucket, and weapon or armor attachments. Ask staff what your region requires.

    Touch Options on the Players HUD to open the Setup HUD (or close it). The first time you wear the HUD without a character, the Setup panel opens on its own.

    Attach the HUD — don't rez it

    The HUD only works when worn. If you drop it on the ground it can't track your character.

    Accept the Experience Permission¶

    The first time you wear the HUD on an Experience-enabled region, Second Life shows a permission popup for the Feudalism Experience. Accept it.

    This permission lets the HUD save your XP, vitals, and character data. Without it, nothing you do will be saved. If you declined by accident, detach and re-wear the HUD to get the popup again.

    1. Choose Your Identity¶

    Locate the Character Setup HUD on your screen. Follow the blue step bar at the top — Name/Title → Gender → Species → Stats → Classes. You can press Save Character at any point to keep progress.

    If your region has more than one universe, pick the correct one first (some require a registration code from staff).

    Gender Selector¶

    Select your character's physical presentation. Click a portrait in the gallery to view details, then choose Select This Gender.

    Species Registry¶

    Your species dictates your inherent racial modifiers and starting regional alignments. Click a species card, review its traits, then choose Select This Species.

    Humans get a head start

    Creating a Human grants +10 Available Points at default stats — handy for a first character. Other species start tuned by their racial defaults. See Species & Classes.

    Class Training Grounds¶

    Choose your starting archetype path. This determines your initial combat and profession skill scaling — each class sets the maximum you can raise stats to (shown as /max on the Stats tab).

    Open the Classes tab (or continue the step bar) to browse the full class gallery, pick your path, and press Finish.

    2. Name & Title¶

    On the Character tab, enter your Character Name (required) and an optional Title or epithet. These appear on your HUD and in roleplay.

    3. Allocate Your Attributes¶

    On the Stats tab, spend all of your Available Points. The total must reach 0 before you can continue.

    Use the + / − buttons beside each stat. Your chosen class caps how high each attribute can go.

    Earn XP in play, spend points here

    XP is earned in the world (combat, crafting, gathering). Available Points are spent in the Setup HUD. Touch Sync/Update on the Players HUD before opening Setup so your latest XP is reflected.

    4. Confirm You're Ready¶

    When you finish the creation flow, the HUD returns to play mode. Check that your Health, Stamina, and Mana spheres show sensible values. (No mana? Many species and classes simply don't use it — that's normal.)

    Walk up to a gatherable bush, a well, or a workstation and touch it. If the world responds, your character instance is live.

    5. When Something Goes Wrong¶

    It's not working — quick fixes
    • HUD and pouch both attached?
    • Did you accept the Experience permission? Re-wear to retry.
    • Is a character loaded? Open the Setup HUD and select or create one.
    • Are you close enough? Most objects need you within 5–15 m.
    • Right ingredients? Crafting stations check your inventory first.
